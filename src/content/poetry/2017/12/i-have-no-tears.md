title=I Have No Tears
date=2017-12-05 12:33
type=page
cat=poetry
tags=2017,joy,memories,tears
status=published
~~~~~~
I want to cry,
    but I have no tears.
I want to be happy,
    but there is no joy.
I want to forget,
    but the memories linger.
I want to remember,
    but the memories fade.
I want,
    but all I want goes wrong.
