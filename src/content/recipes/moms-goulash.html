title=Mom's Goulash date=2012-07-28 type=page cat=recipe status=draft ~~~~~~

Cooking Time: 25 min. Prep Time: 5-10 min. Servings: 4-6

1 lb. Ground Beef (sirloin, round, chuck, etc.)
1 medium to large onion (I prefer white, but yellow will work)
1 cup elbow macaroni noodles (cooked and drained)
1 16oz can stewed tomatoes
1 16oz can kidney beans
salt (to taste)
black pepper (to taste)
cayenne pepper (to taste)
  • Prepare the elbow macaroni according to package directions. Drain.
  • Brown the ground beef with the onion and green pepper in a skillet. Drain.
  • Add the noodles, tomatoes, and kidney beans to the skillet and heat over medium to low heat stirring occasionally until heated thoroughly.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
